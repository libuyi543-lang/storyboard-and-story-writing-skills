# Example Story Outline

## Premise

A widowed father receives a humanoid robot built from his dead daughter's memories, and reluctantly travels with it to replace its failing body.

## Central Question

Can the father accept the robot as his daughter before the journey turns fatal?

## Three Layers

- Event:
  Can they reach the body-replacement facility and survive the antagonist?
- Emotion:
  Can father and daughter form a real bond?
- Value:
  What makes someone family: biology, memory, soul, or chosen love?

## Five Core Beats

1. Catalyst:
   The robot daughter appears in the father's life.
2. Progress and complications:
   They begin the journey and encounter problems that force cooperation.
3. Reversal:
   A helper figure is exposed as the real threat.
4. Climax:
   Father and daughter must act together against the threat.
5. Release:
   The father finally recognizes the daughter emotionally, even if the outcome carries loss or cost.
