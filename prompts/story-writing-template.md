# Story Writing Prompt Template

Use this template with any AI assistant:

```text
Use the files in `story-writing/` as the primary method guide.

Task:
[Describe your premise, draft, or revision goal.]

Requirements:
- Keep the audience caring about a live central question.
- Identify the story on event, emotion, and value layers.
- Use the workflow in `story-writing/workflow.md`.
- If writing scenes or dialogue, also use `story-writing/scene-and-dialogue.md`.
- End with a concrete output, not just advice.

Output format:
[Choose one]
- logline + central question + stakes
- beat sheet
- scene list
- scene draft
- diagnosis + rewrite plan
```
